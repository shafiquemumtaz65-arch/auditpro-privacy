Privacy Policy

AuditPro - Business Audit Generator | Last Updated: November 26, 2025

Your Privacy Matters: AuditPro is committed to protecting your privacy. We do not collect or store any personal information. All data processing happens locally on your device.

1. Information We Collect

AuditPro collects minimal data necessary to provide audit generation services:

Business Data from Google Maps

  • Business Name: The name of the business you're auditing
  • Business Address: Location information displayed on Google Maps
  • Phone Number: Contact information if publicly available
  • Website URL: Business website if listed on Google Maps
  • Reviews & Ratings: Public review data and star ratings
  • Business Hours: Operating hours if available
  • Business Category: Type of business (e.g., restaurant, salon)

Usage Data (Stored Locally Only)

  • Audit Count: Number of audits generated this month (for free tier limit tracking - 15 audits per month)
  • Install Date: Date extension was installed (for monthly limit reset)

Note: Pro version with additional features is planned for future release. When launched, Pro users may provide email and license key for tier verification, stored locally only.

2. How We Use Your Information

The data collected is used exclusively for the following purposes:

  • Generate Marketing Audits: Business data is sent to OpenAI's API to generate professional audit reports
  • Track Usage Limits: Audit count is stored locally to enforce the 15-audit-per-month limit for free users
  • Improve User Experience: Understanding usage patterns helps us enhance the extension

3. Data Storage & Security

Local Storage Only

All user data is stored locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API. We do not maintain external servers or databases to store your information.

What This Means:

  • Your audit count and usage data never leave your device
  • Data is tied to your Chrome browser profile
  • Uninstalling the extension removes all stored data
  • We cannot access or retrieve your data remotely
  • No user account or registration required

4. Third-Party Services

OpenAI API

AuditPro uses OpenAI's API (GPT-4o-mini) to generate AI-powered audit reports. When you generate an audit:

  • Business data (name, address, reviews, etc.) is sent to OpenAI's servers
  • OpenAI processes this data to generate audit recommendations
  • OpenAI's privacy policy applies to this data processing: OpenAI Privacy Policy
  • We use OpenAI's API in compliance with their terms of service
  • No personal user data is sent to OpenAI - only public business information

Google Maps

AuditPro extracts publicly available business information from Google Maps pages that you visit. We do not interact with Google's APIs directly. All data extracted is already publicly visible on Google Maps.

5. What We DON'T Collect

We respect your privacy. AuditPro does NOT collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, etc.)
  • Personal browsing history or activity
  • Credit card or payment information
  • Location data beyond what's publicly shown on Google Maps
  • Cookies or tracking pixels
  • Analytics or telemetry data
  • Any data from websites other than Google Maps

6. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or share your data with third parties, except:

  • OpenAI API: Public business data is sent to OpenAI for audit generation only (as described above)
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law, we may disclose information to comply with legal obligations

Important: We have no access to your personal data since all usage data is stored locally on your device.

7. User Rights & Control

You have complete control over your data:

  • No Account Needed: Free version requires no sign-up or personal information
  • Access: All data is stored locally in Chrome storage, accessible via browser developer tools
  • Delete: Uninstall the extension or clear Chrome storage to remove all stored data
  • Opt-Out: Simply don't use the extension if you don't agree with this policy
  • Control: You control which businesses to audit - we don't automatically collect anything

8. Children's Privacy

AuditPro is intended for business professionals and is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected with an updated "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of AuditPro after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Chrome Web Store Compliance

AuditPro complies with Chrome Web Store's privacy requirements:

  • We request only necessary permissions (activeTab, storage, scripting, tabs)
  • We clearly disclose all data collection and usage
  • We do not use data for advertising or tracking purposes
  • We maintain transparency about third-party services (OpenAI)
  • All data storage is local - no external servers
  • Users can audit and delete their data at any time
